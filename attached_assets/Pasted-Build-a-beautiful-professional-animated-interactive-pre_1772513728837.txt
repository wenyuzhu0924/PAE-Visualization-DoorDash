Build a beautiful, professional, animated interactive presentation dashboard (web app) for my thesis on DoorDash agentic AI governance. The dashboard must be crisp, high-clarity, projector-ready (use SVG or an equivalent rendering that keeps text sharp). Core goals

I will present from this dashboard live.

Viewers should understand the story without guessing what the chart means.

Users can interact by selecting companies/domains, switching views, and seeing the narrative update.

Layout + design requirements

A modern presentation layout: left navigation (story steps) + main canvas.

A top bar with title + subtitle and a Presentation Mode toggle (full screen, hides controls).

Typography large enough for projection; consistent spacing; no overlapping labels.

Polished animation: smooth transitions between story steps, animated chart updates (enter/exit), subtle card shadows, and responsive layout.

A persistent “Notation / How to read” panel that is always visible or collapsible (no hidden definitions).

Modes

Story Mode: guided sequence with Next/Back buttons and a progress indicator.

Explore Mode: free navigation among modules.

Persist user edits (selected companies/domains and layout) in localStorage.
